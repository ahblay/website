Welcome to this website. On it you will find some of my programming and math projects, and some strange pieces of writing that I've done over the years. If the creative genius of the writing fails to attract you, please refrain from judgment, as doing so will crush my fragile ego. As time moves on and we march steadily towards Earth's imminent and inevitable ecological apocalypse, I may continue to add other forms of content to this site. Perhaps an album of photos of Antarctica (before its ice sheets have melted and flooded our coastal cities) or some sketches and drawings (we might as well enjoy art before we are subsumed into a fiery hellscape). I would like to even include some interactive and educational articles, perhaps in a futile effort to color our otherwise bleak future with some shades of optimism. Above all, however, this site seves as a marketing tool, so if you like my work and need to hire a programmer/math guy who's concerned about the current state of the earth, please reach out.
