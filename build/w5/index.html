Motivating QUESTIONS
I feel as though I am most motivated to learn by examining, in depth, questions or topics that may seem, at the surface, innocuous or otherwise uninteresting. Questions like "what is speed?", to explore the basic ideas of calculus. I figure that I might as well jot down questions like this when they occur to me (or are asked of me). That's what this is for.
